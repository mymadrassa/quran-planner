Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before enrolling

Do I need any experience to start? ▼
No experience is needed at all. We welcome complete beginners of all ages. Your tutor will assess your level in the first session and build a plan just for you.
How do the online sessions work? ▼
Sessions are held live via Zoom or Google Meet. You will receive a link before each class. All you need is a device with a camera and microphone.
Can I choose my session times? ▼
Yes! When you enrol, you let us know your preferred days and times and we will match you with a tutor who fits your schedule.
Is there a trial or free session? ▼
You can book a free 30-minute consultation call to meet us and ask any questions before committing to a plan.
What age groups do you teach? ▼
We teach all ages — from young children (age 4+) to adults and reverts. Each lesson is fully personalised to the student.
How do I pay? ▼
After your enrolment request is confirmed, we will send you a payment link. We accept bank transfer, card, and other methods.
